About Us

Our idea "Swo Bali" is a complete guide for the farmers that helps by assisting them through their crop selection. The idea proposed by our system is to "Make farmers and soil work better not harder." This motto of our system can be achieved by implementing precision farming through our application by introducing the use of the right crop and fertilizer through data analysis.

Maximization of yields through harvesting suitable crops every time also promotes crop rotation and helps preserve the nutrients in the soil. Prediction of crops can also help the government organization to make appropriate planning beforehand like storing, selling, and in minimizing the import and increasing the export in the long run.
